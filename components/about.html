About EcoWatt

Our Mission

At EcoWatt, we're on a mission to revolutionize home energy management. We believe that by providing homeowners with the tools to understand and control their energy consumption, we can create a more sustainable future for all.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every home is energy-efficient, where consumers are empowered with real-time data and insights, and where reducing our carbon footprint is as simple as checking your smartphone.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, EcoWatt emerged from a simple idea: make energy consumption visible and manageable for everyone. Our team of passionate engineers and designers worked tirelessly to create a device that's both powerful and user-friendly.
